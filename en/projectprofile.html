PROJECT PROFILE

Our business fields are conception, planning, design, research, and operation.
We will demonstrate a full range of construction management capabilities for project realization in all fields.

  • ・We will respond with the latest technology ahead of the times
  • ・Safety and security are fundamental. We will respond with advanced technology that protects people.
  • ・We will respond with human-friendly technology suitable for the age of lifelong learning.
  • ・We will pursue ideals, and respond with technologies that are friendly to all living things and the global environment.
  • ・We will respond with low-cost, concrete and high-quality technology.
  • ・We will bring together the power of the Mitsui Group and respond with comprehensive technology.

Rivers and Sabo

We are engaged in water disaster prevention such as making various plans for water control,
water utilization and environment, and creating flood hazard maps.

Roads and Bridges

We provide consulting on general road maintenance such as road planning and maintenance planning,
design, and survey.

Ports, Airports and Coasts

We consistently carry out surveys, plans, and designs related to ports, and provide total design as the gateway to the ocean.

Urban Planning

Keeping in mind the pillars of technology, ecology and tradition, we are working on various projects for community development.

Drainage and Sewage

We are working on the planning and design of sewerage facilities, which are a social infrastructure lifeline.

Ground Analysis

When designing for harsh conditions such as soft ground, we conduct analysis and plan countermeasures to safely construct the facility.

Environment and RenewableEnergy

We carry out surveys, forecasts, and conservation measures in various fields such as cities, rivers, sea areas, and harbors.

Telecommunications

We plan,research, and design management systems for dams, floodgates, roads, etc.

InformationSystems

We design and develop information systems that contribute to the construction industry, such as disaster countermeasure support systems.

Conservation

In order to extend the service life of various facilities, we carry out a series of operations from inspection to investigation and reinforcement design.

Management(CM/PPP/PFI)

We are engaged in CM / PPP, client support, designated administrator work, etc.

Overseas Projects

We provide consulting services on nation-building support and infrastructure development such as ODA,centered on developing countries.

Message from the President

Mitsui Consultants Co., Ltd. was established in 1965 as an independent and total engineering consulting firm, with a joint capital investment from the twenty companies of the Mitsui Group. Since its inception, our firm has strived to implement public inftastructure and facilities projects of the highest quality, with our eyes constantly set on the direction and the needs of the next generation. We are considered today one of the premier engineering consulting firms in Japan.

In recent years, the socio-economic environment in Japan and many parts of the globe has shifted greatly, and accordingly, the diversification of values and perspectives has been significant among the stakeholders and end users. In pursuit of public asset development, the unquestioned preference for the perpetual infrastructure expansion has come to an end, and the transparency in terms of purpose, function, implementation method and financial feasibility are strongly demanded of any project. Furthermore, the maintenance and optimization of the existing infrastructure stock have become the highest priority in order to prolong longevity and to maximize value of the given infrastructure investment. In these transforming circumstances, our basic motto is. "Contribution to Society through Technical Expertise". Under the phrase "MCC for Humanity and Environment", we continuously aim to expand and deepen our accumulated consulting knowledge in engineering and environmental design through practical focus on development and acquisition of new technology. Our ultimate and shared goals are to preserve our beautiful Earth and to ensure safe, secure and prosperous lives for the people.

On this milestone occasion, we remind ourselves that the engineering consultants must not only pursue technical excellence but also strive to nurture and sustain professional purpose and ethics, individually and as an expert group. We pledge not only to provide technical solutions of the highest order, but also to remind ourselves to stand in the shoes of the clients and end users, and to think and act with foresight for the future, in order to be a truly effective and trusted group of experts for the betterment of societies around the globe.

MCC-Vision100

- Mission -

Creation of a Prosperous and Sustainable Earth Society

Our Concept of "Earth Society" Places Humanity Among the Ecosystem of All Living Flora, Fauna and Inorganic Components that Constitute Our Living Sphere.

- Our Objectives -

To be a Steward of Earth Habitat for Humanity and Beyond

Today, Global Society is Under Threat from a Wide Range of Existential Problems, Including Energy and Resources Scarcity, Environmental Degradation, Worsening Natural Disasters, and Increasing Socioeconomic Stratification.

We, as Engineering Consultants, Possess the Accumulated Knowledge on Built and Natural Environment and the Flow of Information therein, as well as the Conceptualizing Skills to Identify and Analyze Complex Problems, Formulate Strategies, and Design and Implement Workable Solutions.

We will Spare No Effort to Deepen Our Knowledge and Conceptual Creativity, to Remain Focused on Long Term Visions for the Earth Society, and to Pass on Our Civilizational Inheritance and Healthy Ecosystem to the Future Generations.

- Corporate Strengths to Achieve Our Objective -

1. Focus on Creativity and Technical Innovation

Toward the Realization of a Sustainable Earth Society, We Maximize Our Creativity through Knowledge, Conceptual Skills and Technical Innovation.

2. No Compromise on Quality and Ethics

Quality is Our Competitiveness. Ethics is Our Value. These are Our Guiding Principles in Every Endeavor.

3. Nurturing of Expert Professionals with Depth of Intelligence and Courage to Act

We Nurture Growth of Young Professionals with Depth of Knowledge and Intelligence, Who are Inspired and Fearless In Challenging New Horizons In Pursuit of Our Mission.

4. Creation of Work Environment Where Each of Us Shines and Takes Pride in What We Do

In Response to Diversified Life Priorities and Preferred Working Arrangements, We Strive to Create an Accommodative Work Place Where Each of Us Can Commit to One's Career and Remain Vibrant Collectively.

- Aspiration -

To be at the Conceptual and Technical Frontier of Formulating Future Earth Society

Contribution to Prosperous and Sustainable Earth Society. To be a Leader under This Purpose with Technical Excellence and Accumulated Intelligence.
